What's the difference between web design and web development?
In a general term, Web design is the creation of websites and pages to reflect a company's brand and information and ensure a user-friendly experience. Appearance and design are incorporated as vital elements whether you're

designing a website, mobile app or maintaining content on a web page.
In other words web design is act of deigning and rendering of contents to the web site or web pages.
While Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.
